Worship House lead pastor and prominent gospel singer, Pastor Wilson Bugembe has today announced his partnership with Bukedde TV ahead of his concert next Friday.
Bukedde TV will aid in promoting the pastor’s “Katonda Yabade Mweno Ensonga” concert, using his massive reach. The pastor also announced that all gate collections will proceed to roofing his 6,000 seater church in Nansana.

Pastor Wilson Bugembe will be staging a worship music concert at Hotel Africana slated for 3rd June, on Martyrs Day at Hotel Africana before proceeding to other areas. The concert has been organised by CRK planet limited.
